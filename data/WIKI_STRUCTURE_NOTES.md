# Actual Wiki Structure Findings

## Two Types of Character Pages

### Type 1: Major Characters (Rand, Egwene, Mat, etc.)

- NO temporal "## In [Book]" sections
- Instead: Comprehensive topical sections
  - Overview
  - Appearance  
  - Channeling abilities
  - Family and Ancestry
  - Relationships
  - etc.
- References to books inline within sections

### Type 2: Minor Characters

- HAVE temporal "## In [Book]" sections
- Less detailed than major characters
- Content organized by book appearance

## All Pages Have

- Complete infobox (biographical, physical, chronological)
- Categories (99%)
- Detailed content
